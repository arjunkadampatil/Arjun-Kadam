Introduction to Computers


Faculty - Dr. Shankho Subhra Pal & Dr. Achal Agrawal


The Introduction to Computer course at Sitare University provides students with a foundational understanding of computer tools, covering topics like excel, word, and web development. It equips students with essential skills for web-development and web-design in the digital age.

First 5 weeks - Dr. Achal Agrawal

  • MS Excel:

    • A spreadsheet program used for data analysis, calculations, and creating charts, essential for organizing and managing numerical data.

  • MS Word:

    • A word processing tool for creating, editing, and formatting text documents, widely used for professional writing and documentation.

  • MS PowerPoint:

    • A presentation software used to design and display slideshows, combining text, images, and multimedia for impactful communication.

  • Linux Commands:

    • A set of instructions in Linux operating systems to manage files, processes, and system operations through a command-line interface.
